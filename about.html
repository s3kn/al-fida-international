About Us – AL Fida International

AL Fida International is a trusted spices and agricultural export company based in India, committed to delivering high-quality, fresh, and hygienically processed products to global and local markets. With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction, we serve clients across Bangladesh, Vietnam, Sri Lanka, as well as domestic markets in India.
We specialize in the sourcing and export of a wide range of spices and vegetables, carefully selected from reliable farms and processed under strict quality standards to preserve freshness, aroma, and nutritional value.

Our core product range includes:

  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Potato
  • Red Chili
  • Turmeric
  • Drumstick
  • Tamarind Seeds
  • Various Vegetables and Spices
At AL Fida International, we believe in building long-term relationships through transparency, timely delivery, and uncompromised quality. Our efficient supply chain, export expertise, and commitment to international standards enable us to meet the diverse needs of our customers worldwide. With a vision to expand our global presence, AL Fida International continues to grow as a reliable name in the agricultural and spices export industry.
